# Tier 5 - Calamities (Levels 141-150)

> Endgame calamities, primordial forces, sealing-era horrors, and campaign-ending entities.

## Role In The Campaign

Tier 5 creatures are not standard encounters. They represent apocalyptic thresholds, final truths, and victory conditions that often extend beyond combat.

## Common Themes

- reality distortion
- sealing logic
- multi-stage resolution
- extreme narrative consequence
- world-defining aftermath

## Representative Creatures

- Echo of Vor'Athek
- Primordial Dragon
- Voice of the Sealing
- Manifested Vor'Athek

## Encounter Guidance

Use Tier 5 for:

- final arc confrontations
- sealing or unsealing decisions
- the collapse of entire regions
- endgame faction realignment
- campaign resolution scenes

## Reward Profile

Typical rewards include:

- singular artifacts
- permanent titles or marks
- large-scale world stabilization or ruin
- irreversible truth
- ending-state control

## GM Notes

Tier 5 should never feel routine. If one appears, the campaign is already standing at the edge of transformation.
