# Tier 4 - Lords (Levels 111-140)

> Warping entities, ancient rulers, void-adjacent horrors, and endgame-scale guardians.

## Role In The Campaign

Tier 4 creatures should be rare, heavily signaled, and tied to major campaign locations or irreversible choices.

## Common Themes

- arena warping
- legendary resistance patterns
- ritual or world-state interaction
- faction panic
- high lethality with preparation windows

## Representative Creatures

- Abyss Dragon
- Ancient Lich
- Lord of Specters
- Khorrath Colossus
- Primordial Chimera
- Corrupted Keth Titan
- Devourer of Seals
- Heart of Ashes Guardian

## Encounter Guidance

Use Tier 4 for:

- late-campaign boss fights
- sealed-zone incursions
- climax preparation arcs
- catastrophic route denial
- truth-reveal encounters with heavy cost

## Reward Profile

Typical rewards include:

- legendary materials
- access to sealing knowledge
- world-state shifts
- unique artifacts
- irreversible political fallout

## GM Notes

Tier 4 should make survival itself feel like an achievement.
