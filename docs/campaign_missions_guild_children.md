# Guild of Threads and Children of the Broken Thread Missions

Extracted from `campaign_missions.md` and normalized to English for portfolio and runtime reference.

## Guild of Threads Missions

### `guild_01` - The Intact Sample

**Minimum reputation:** 0 (open mission)

**Objective:** Capture or recover an intact Thorn-Gray specimen (Tier 2) from the plains near the Isles of Myr. "Intact" means the creature cannot be killed by magic - only by physical means or live capture.

**Hidden agenda:** The Guild wants to study how corruption has advanced through this bloodline over the last fifty years. They possess historical records and are comparing the speed of deterioration, which is accelerating in line with the 3% report. They do not want anyone else to know they are investigating it.

**Location:** Outer plains of the Isles of Myr, roughly one hour from Port Myr.

**Key NPC:** Researcher Lena Vor, a Guild contact in Port Myr operating under the cover of an environmental naturalist.

**Rewards:**
- `guild_of_threads`: +15
- 80 gold and an Alchemist Kit
- Access to the Guild's public archive in Port Myr, including basic Keth crafting recipes

**Conflict:**
- `church_pure_flame`: -5

**Unlocks:** `guild_02`

---

### `guild_02` - The Fissure

**Minimum reputation:** +20 with `guild_of_threads`

**Objective:** Escort Lena Vor to the Fissure in the Broken Square so she can take measurements. The mission requires four uninterrupted hours of night work - the Myr Council cannot know, and the Church definitely cannot know.

**Hidden agenda:** Lena carries a fractured Crystal of Silence, a lesser fragment of the original Vel'Ossian artifact. When brought near the Fissure, it reacts violently because the Fissure amplifies it. The Guild needs to know whether the site is a resonance point of Vor'Athek or an anchor point of the Sealing. The test will answer that question definitively, but it may trigger a visible magical event in Port Myr.

**Location:** Broken Square, Port Myr, at night.

**Key NPCs:** Lena Vor and Seyla Vorn, who may help with chemical containment if the players already earned her trust.

**Rewards:**
- `guild_of_threads`: +25
- 200 gold
- Lena reveals that the Guild has known about the Sealing's collapse for two years and knows the Last Chamber exists

**Conflict:**
- `church_pure_flame`: -15 if the magical disturbance becomes public
- `myr_council`: -10 because unauthorized magical incidents inside neutral territory are not tolerated

**Unlocks:** `guild_03`

---

### `guild_03` - The Third Floor

**Minimum reputation:** +40 with `guild_of_threads`

**Objective:** Travel to Vel'Ossian, Isle of Mirrors, and access the third floor of the Guild headquarters - a floor sealed since 4213. The Guild provides the means of entry. What must be done there will only be revealed once the party arrives.

**Hidden agenda:** The third floor contains a partially translated Aeridian Manuscript describing the exact mechanics of the Incomplete Sealing. The translation reveals that the Sealing was designed to be replaced by a Permanent Sealing, and that the Last Chamber contains the components needed to perform it. The Guild wants the Travelers to read the manuscript and decide what to do. The Guild cannot make that decision for them, for narrative reasons that are still unclear even to its own masters.

**Location:** Vel'Ossian, Isle of Mirrors, about a day and a half by ship from Port Myr.

**Key NPC:** Master Archivist Sorel, the only Guild master who knows the fullest version of the truth.

**Rewards:**
- `guild_of_threads`: +35
- Full access to the Guild archive, including all crafting recipes and all languages at half cost
- Sorel reveals that the Last Chamber responds to the intention of the one who enters it, not merely to the ritual performed there

**Conflict:**
- `church_pure_flame`: -20
- `empire_valdrek`: -10 because the Kael Archive monitors visitors to Vel'Ossian

**Unlocks:** Full Guild narrative arc and access to the Guild's version of the Last Chamber secret

## Children of the Broken Thread Missions

### `children_01` - The Quiet Package

**Minimum reputation:** 0 (open mission)

**Objective:** Deliver a sealed package to an address in the Vel Alleys without opening it, asking questions, or logging the transaction. Thresh pays in advance.

**Hidden agenda:** The package contains a classified map of imperial Keth transport routes obtained through infiltration of an Archive outpost. The Children use that information to intercept cargo and resell it to finance their operations. Thresh is testing whether the players are capable of discreet work and whether they will report the operation to the Archive.

**Location:** Port Myr, Vel Alleys.

**Key NPC:** Thresh.

**Rewards:**
- `children_of_broken_thread`: +15
- 50 gold
- Thresh opens an honest conversation about who the Children are and what they want

**Conflict:**
- `empire_valdrek`: -10 if the delivery is exposed

**Unlocks:** `children_02`

---

### `children_02` - The Extraction

**Minimum reputation:** +15 with `children_of_broken_thread`

**Objective:** Rescue a prisoner from a temporary Church detention center in Port Myr before transfer to Sanctum for Purification. The party has a 36-hour window. Thresh provides the layout of the site but cannot participate personally because he is too well known.

**Hidden agenda:** The prisoner is a corrupted survivor named Aveth who endured three consecutive magical Surges without losing consciousness, something that should not be possible. He carries fragmented knowledge about how to interact with Vor'Athek without being consumed. The Children believe that knowledge is the key to their planned Stabilization Ritual. The Church wants Aveth's capacity, not Aveth himself.

**Location:** Temporary Church detention center in Port Myr, a repurposed warehouse near Pier Three.

**Key NPCs:** Thresh during planning, Aveth during the rescue.

**Rewards:**
- `children_of_broken_thread`: +25
- 150 gold
- Aveth shares this warning: "Vor'Athek does not want to destroy. It wants to complete something. I do not know what. But I can feel it waiting."

**Conflict:**
- `church_pure_flame`: -25
- `empire_valdrek`: -10 because imperial guards cooperate with the Church at the site

**Unlocks:** `children_03`

---

### `children_03` - Ritual in the Pale Belt

**Minimum reputation:** +35 with `children_of_broken_thread`

**Objective:** Escort Thresh and a Children cell to ruins on the edge of the Pale Belt, the Void Zone between Valdoria and Estravar, and protect a ritual he describes as stabilizing the corrupted Thread in the area.

**Hidden agenda:** The ritual does not stabilize anything. Thresh knows that. It is an attempt to amplify instability in a controlled way to force a localized Surge that would prove publicly and irreversibly that the Sealing is failing. The Children believe the only way to move the great factions toward a real solution is to make the crisis impossible to deny. Thresh is willing to cause a lesser Surge to achieve that. Aveth will try to warn the party before the ritual begins.

**Location:** The Pale Belt, in the buried ruins of a partially swallowed Aeridian city, about three travel days from Port Myr.

**Key NPCs:** Thresh, Aveth, and the Weaver, who appears because she already knew the ritual would be attempted.

**Rewards if the players assist without intervening:**
- `children_of_broken_thread`: +30
- Thresh reveals the faction's true objective: forcing the opening of the Last Chamber before total collapse
- The Weaver speaks directly to the players for the first time

**Alternative rewards if the players stop the Surge:**
- `children_of_broken_thread`: -15
- The Weaver still appears and approves of the party's decision
- Thresh accepts the verdict and begins discussing an alternative path

**Conflict:**
- Any faction that learns what happened in the Pale Belt reacts, each in a different way

**Unlocks:** Full Children narrative arc and the Weaver as an active character in the story
