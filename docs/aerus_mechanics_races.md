## VIII. Playable Races

### Global Rules

- **Total stat sum:** 60 points per subrace.
- **Special cap:** One attribute may reach 15.
- **Standard cap:** No other attribute may exceed 12.
- **Starting gear:** combat item + utility item + racial identity item.

### The Three-Pillar Racial Framework

- **Virtus Entis** - the innate gift.
- **Onus Entis** - the biological or spiritual burden.
- **Conceptus Entis** - the code of being.

---

### Race 1: Humans

**Universal trait:** Once per session, a human may ignore one racial penalty affecting the group through stubborn adaptability.

#### 1.1 Northerners (Valdoria)

`STR 11 | DEX 10 | INT 12 | VIT 11 | LUK 8 | CAR 8`

- **Virtus Entis - Ash Memory:** On first contact with a ruin, faction, or institution, the GM provides one true cultural fact as passive inherited memory.
- **Onus Entis - System Rigidity:** In chaotic magic zones, suffer `-2` on magical rolls.
- **Conceptus Entis - Protocol:** Defined by three family rules established at creation.

Starting items:
- Valdorian short sword
- House affiliation registry
- Family copper insignia

#### 1.2 Traders (Isles of Myr)

`STR 8 | DEX 12 | INT 10 | VIT 9 | LUK 15 | CAR 6`

- **Virtus Entis - Thread Drift:** Once per session, redirect a Blooming toward a chosen target. Gain bonus luck on negotiation-resolved loot scenes.
- **Onus Entis - Shallow Roots:** Outside the Isles of Myr, suffer permanent effective luck loss until returning.
- **Conceptus Entis - Given Word:** A public promise is binding.

Starting items:
- Myr knife
- Mixed purse
- Ring of Word

#### 1.3 Khorathi (Khorrath)

`STR 10 | DEX 11 | INT 9 | VIT 15 | LUK 8 | CAR 7`

- **Virtus Entis - Body Without Thread:** Function normally in low-magic absence zones and resist corruption better than most.
- **Onus Entis - Structural Skepticism:** Incoming healing magic is less effective.
- **Conceptus Entis - Economy of Necessity:** Every carried item must justify its place.

Starting items:
- Short gray-arc spear
- Desert survival kit
- Desert ash vial

#### 1.4 Dawnmere Folk (Shaleth Protectorate)

`STR 9 | DEX 11 | INT 11 | VIT 9 | LUK 10 | CAR 10`

- **Virtus Entis - Echo of the Thread:** Sense the direction of recently used magic nearby and gain easier early magical learning.
- **Onus Entis - Between Worlds:** Mild social penalties in both imperial and elven spaces depending on context.
- **Conceptus Entis - Learned Courtesy:** Adopt three observable social rules from an elven subrace at creation.

Starting items:
- Vel'Arath shortbow
- Protectorate pass
- Tradition fragment

### Race 2: Elves and Fae

**Universal trait:** Every 2 points invested into STR costs 1 additional permanent VIT. Elven bodies are not built for brute force.

#### 2.1 Twilight Elves

`STR 7 | DEX 12 | INT 15 | VIT 9 | LUK 8 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Thread Sight:** Detect active magic, identify recent Surge scars, and see magical beings as luminous silhouettes in darkness.
- **Onus Entis - Body of Light:** Physical fragility remains the central limitation.
- **Conceptus Entis - House Tradition:** Publicly breaking core tradition changes how other elves treat you.

Starting items:
- Singing blade of Vel'Arath
- Thread spyglass
- Amber house sigil

#### 2.2 Corrupted Fae

`STR 7 | DEX 12 | INT 12 | VIT 9 | LUK 11 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Reading Corruption:** Automatically identify weaknesses and immunities in corrupted beings.
- **Onus Entis - Inner Fracture:** Lose permanent vitality over long progression milestones.
- **Conceptus Entis - Silence About Origin:** Share your true sight only after ritual trust.

Starting items:
- Twisted Thread dagger
- Corruption journal
- Veil of origin

#### 2.3 Mist Elves

`STR 7 | DEX 12 | INT 11 | VIT 9 | LUK 12 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Voice of the Forest:** Gain instinctive warning of hostility in forests and may ask the forest about events nearby once per session.
- **Onus Entis - Double Root:** Cities drain them over time.
- **Conceptus Entis - The Threshold:** Every border crossed should be announced aloud.

Starting items:
- Threshold longbow
- Threshold seed pouch
- Staff of announcement

#### 2.4 Wandering Fae

`STR 7 | DEX 15 | INT 10 | VIT 9 | LUK 10 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Thread Without Anchor:** Learn low-tier magic by observation and resist illusions through luck.
- **Onus Entis - Spiritual Exile:** Without a defined home, penalties accumulate after extended drift.
- **Conceptus Entis - Self-Made Tradition:** Begin with two rules and add more as your story progresses.

Starting items:
- Twin dagger set
- Incomplete map
- Tradition journal

### Race 3: Forgers

**Universal trait:** Resistant to displacement and knockback, but always worse at stealth.

#### 3.1 Stone Goliaths

`STR 15 | DEX 7 | INT 9 | VIT 12 | LUK 8 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Memory of Stone:** Read emotional residue from natural stone and Keth.
- **Onus Entis - Literal Weight:** Swimming, falling, and precision motion are harder.
- **Conceptus Entis - Calcification:** Important life events are ritually carved into the body.

Starting items:
- Keth mace
- Chisel and hammer
- First calcification mark

#### 3.2 Deep Dwarves

`STR 11 | DEX 9 | INT 12 | VIT 12 | LUK 8 | CAR 8`

- **Virtus Entis - Sense of Keth:** Automatically detect Keth, Fragments, and fossilized ether in short range.
- **Onus Entis - Hostile Surface:** Perception worsens under open daylight.
- **Conceptus Entis - The Craft:** Once per session, solve a practical obstacle through your hereditary trade.

Starting items:
- Pure Keth axe
- Trade kit
- Engraved clan insignia

#### 3.3 Stenvaard Smiths

`STR 12 | DEX 8 | INT 10 | VIT 12 | LUK 9 | CAR 9`

- **Virtus Entis - Living Forge:** Extreme cold resistance and the ability to superheat touched metal once per session.
- **Onus Entis - Furnace Temper:** Emotionally slower to calm after conflict.
- **Conceptus Entis - Forged Oath:** Major promises are treated as forged work orders, not casual speech.

Starting items:
- Stenvaard forge hammer
- Portable tongs and maintenance tools
- Temper mark token

### Race 4: Chimerics

Chimerics are living expressions of unstable lineage, magical adaptation, or engineered ancestry. Their greatest strength is flexibility, and their greatest danger is instability.

Typical design principles:

- higher variance between subraces;
- more narrative interaction with corruption, mutation, or resonance;
- stronger unique utility in exchange for social distrust.

The GM should treat Chimerics as mechanically legal but socially loaded characters.

---

### Race 5: Myth-Born

Myth-Born are the rarest playable ancestries in Aerus. They feel less like ethnic groups and more like mythological survivals made flesh.

Typical design principles:

- exceptional narrative power;
- strong thematic burden;
- rare recognition, fear, or reverence from NPCs;
- consequences that scale with visibility.

Use them when the campaign tone supports extraordinary origin without reducing the grim stakes of the setting.
