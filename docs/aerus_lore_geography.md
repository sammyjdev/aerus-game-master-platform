### Valdoria

**Climate:** Temperate. Broad rivers. Fertile central plains and mountains to the west. The soil contains veins of fossilized ether, which makes magic slightly more stable here.

**Kingdoms:**

| Name | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Valdrek | Empire | Capital: Auramveld. Controls 60% of Valdoria |
| Principalities of Osthara | Confederation | Seven principalities in a defensive alliance |
| Ferren March | Vassal territory | Technically autonomous, effectively occupied |
| Free Territory of Calmveth | Merchant republic | The busiest port in Valdoria |

**Notable places:**

- **Auramveld:** Imperial capital built over Aeridian ruins. Divided into the High City, Middle City, and the Low Flats. The palace known as **the Spine** has an uncharted understructure.
- **Gorath Fissures:** Canyons with localized time distortions. Travelers sometimes see versions of themselves from hours earlier. The Guild maintains **Gray Post 7** on the edge.
- **Ondrek Pass:** The only safe land route to Estravar. A 3 km Aeridian tunnel with no magic inside. Kethara charges tolls in Keth.

### Shaleth

**Climate:** Hot and humid in the south. The Forest of Vel'Arath covers nearly 40% of the continent.

**Notable places:**

- **Vel'Arath:** An ancestral forest claimed by no kingdom. The Thread there feels ancient rather than corrupted, as if Vor'Athek never touched it.
- **Threshold of Vel'Arath:** Compasses spin uselessly and time appears to move more slowly.

### Estravar

**Climate:** Arctic. Near the pole, nights can last six months.

**Notable places:**

- **Keth Veins:** A unique mineral network that absorbs and stabilizes corrupted Thread energy. Every high-quality armor set in Aerus contains some Keth.
- **Pale Belt:** The Void Zone separating Estravar from Valdoria. Permanent fog, pervasive danger, and only one truly safe crossing through Ondrek Pass.

### Khorrath

**Climate:** Arid to extremely arid. Sandstorms carry arcane particulates.

**Notable places:**

- **Arcane Ash Desert:** The epicenter of the ritual. Black vitrified ground remains warm just below the surface.
- **Heart of Ashes:** A 40 km crater. At its center stands **the Last Chamber**, an Aeridian crystal structure the size of a city.

### Veth

**Climate:** Humid tropical south, savanna to the north.

**Notable places:**

- **Forbidden Interior:** Even the Vethans do not enter it. Maps show only silence.
- **Red Sea:** The ocean between Veth and Khorrath, rust-red from lingering arcane residue.

### Isles of Myr

The isles remain neutral under the **Pact of Myr** and are administered by the **Myr Council**. The Thread here is chaotic but not corrupted, like an out-of-tune instrument rather than a broken one. Magic still works, but often produces harmless side effects known as **Bloomings**.

**Cities:**

| City | Island | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Port Myr | Great Isle of Myr | Commercial capital and the campaign starting point |
| Vel'Ossian | Isle of Mirrors | Headquarters of the Guild of Threads |
| Sanctum | Flame Isle | Headquarters of the Church of the Pure Flame |

**Port Myr districts:**

- **Five Flags Docks:** Each nation keeps its own dedicated pier.
- **Broken Square:** Commercial center with a 40 cm fissure pulsing blue light. Excavation is forbidden by decree.
- **Vel Alleys:** A permanent black market. Guards patrol the perimeter but never go in.
- **Council Tower:** Administrative headquarters. The eighth floor appears on old blueprints but not on current ones.
