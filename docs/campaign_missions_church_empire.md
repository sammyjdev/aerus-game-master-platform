# Campaign Missions - Church And Empire

> Mission set for the Church of the Pure Flame and the Empire of Valdrek.

---

## Church Of The Pure Flame

### `church_01` - The Unlicensed Mage

- Minimum reputation: 0
- Objective: locate and report an unlicensed magic user operating in Port Myr
- Hidden agenda: the true target is tied to knowledge the Church wants suppressed before it reaches the Guild
- Function: introduces seal politics, Church oversight, and moral compromise

### `church_02` - The Heretical Text

- Minimum reputation: +20 with `church_pure_flame`
- Objective: recover a manuscript said to deny the sanctity of the purified Thread
- Hidden agenda: the document is politically dangerous because it reframes control as doctrine
- Function: tests loyalty to the Church against truth, knowledge, and faction trust

### `church_03` - The Purification

- Minimum reputation: +35 with `church_pure_flame`
- Objective: attend or assist a public purification rite in Sanctum
- Hidden agenda: the ritual extracts value under the language of sanctity
- Function: escalates the cost of alliance with the Church and clarifies its worldview

---

## Empire Of Valdrek

### `valdrek_01` - The Smuggler's Ledger

- Minimum reputation: 0
- Objective: escort an accounting book to the imperial customs tower in Port Myr
- Hidden agenda: the Empire cares as much about discretion and leverage as it does about the evidence itself
- Function: introduces imperial intelligence style and Port Myr political friction

### `valdrek_02` - Military Keth

- Minimum reputation: +15 with `empire_valdrek`
- Objective: investigate who is moving military-grade Keth outside imperial oversight
- Hidden agenda: the leak may originate inside the Empire itself
- Function: reveals imperial fragmentation and strategic paranoia

### `valdrek_03` - The Sealed Letter

- Minimum reputation: +30 with `empire_valdrek`
- Objective: deliver a sealed letter from a trusted imperial intermediary to Auramveld
- Hidden agenda: the letter changes how the Empire understands the Travelers
- Function: turns the party from local anomaly into strategic political variable

---

## Design Notes

- Church missions should emphasize purity, control, doctrine, and moral pressure.
- Empire missions should emphasize order, intelligence, bureaucracy, and strategic manipulation.
- Neither faction should feel cartoonishly evil; both should feel convinced they are preserving necessary stability.
- Mission rewards should include reputation shifts, access, information, and future leverage rather than money alone.
