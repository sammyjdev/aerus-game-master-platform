# Tier 3 - Predators (Levels 71-110)

> Apex hunters, elite corrupted beings, ancient guardians, and regional nightmares.

## Role In The Campaign

Tier 3 creatures are no longer routine monsters. Their presence changes local behavior, faction strategy, and travel expectations.

## Common Themes

- strong battlefield identity
- multi-layered attacks
- stronger legendary behavior
- environmental distortion
- durable narrative impact

## Representative Creatures

- Ash Dragon
- Corrupted Angel
- Ancient Vampire
- Storm Dragon
- Awakened Archmage
- Leviathan of Myr
- Shadow Manticore
- Red River Kraken

## Encounter Guidance

Use Tier 3 for:

- major arc milestones
- regional boss fights
- forbidden expeditions
- faction-backed hunts
- late-midgame catastrophe warnings

## Reward Profile

Typical rewards include:

- rare and epic materials
- major faction reputation shifts
- access to high-risk regions
- ritual components
- campaign-altering information

## GM Notes

Tier 3 creatures should feel like remembered events, not just difficult stat blocks.
