# Tier 1 - Scum (Levels 1-30)

> Early-game threats, scavengers, unstable wildlife, and low-tier corrupted creatures.

## Role In The Campaign

Tier 1 creatures establish danger without overwhelming the party. They are ideal for first contact with corruption, travel ambushes, urban outskirts, and survival pressure.

## Common Themes

- swarming behavior
- unstable mutation
- local predation
- low-grade corruption
- resource-driven encounters

## Representative Creatures

- Graythorn
- Surge Goblin
- Shade-Turner
- Keth Beast
- Hungry Specter
- Broken Construct
- Ash Harpy
- Belt Wolf

## Encounter Guidance

Use Tier 1 for:

- first expeditions outside safe zones
- city-edge danger
- road and trail encounters
- faction errands that turn violent
- corruption foreshadowing

## Reward Profile

Typical rewards include:

- common crafting reagents
- minor weapons or salvage
- clues about a region
- early reputation changes
- hints about stronger local threats

## GM Notes

Tier 1 should feel dangerous to the unprepared, but readable. The goal is pressure, not annihilation.
