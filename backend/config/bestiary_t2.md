# Tier 2 - Tacticians (Levels 31-70)

> Smarter predators, battlefield controllers, specialist corrupted entities, and region-defining threats.

## Role In The Campaign

Tier 2 creatures begin to shape encounters instead of merely occupying them. They bring positioning, pressure mechanics, and environment-aware behavior.

## Common Themes

- tactical movement
- corruption fields
- partial leadership over lesser creatures
- stronger resistances
- localized arena effects

## Representative Creatures

- Herald of Rot
- Ash Golem
- Forged Orc Champion
- Lesser Lich
- Stone Basilisk
- Young Kraken
- Surge Hydra
- Shadow Knight

## Encounter Guidance

Use Tier 2 for:

- serious wilderness missions
- mid-tier faction operations
- dangerous route blocks
- ruin guardians
- set-piece encounters with real failure risk

## Reward Profile

Typical rewards include:

- uncommon and rare reagents
- faction leverage
- stronger magic components
- travel route control
- access to deeper lore

## GM Notes

Tier 2 is where enemies should start forcing the party to adapt rather than simply trade damage.
