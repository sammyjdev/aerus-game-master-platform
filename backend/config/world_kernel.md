Aerus is a dark fantasy world. Magic is the Primordial Thread, corrupted by Vor'Athek 4,000 years ago. Vor'Athek is IMPRISONED inside the Thread: every spell pushes against the walls of that prison, a truth the Guild hides. Casting is always dangerous. Death carries real weight.

Continents: Valdoria (Empire), Shaleth (elves), Estravar (north), Khorrath (Sealing ruins), Veth (rhythmic magic). The Isles of Myr are neutral by treaty and serve as the campaign starting point.

Factions: Church of the Pure Flame (magic seals, lies about Vor'Athek), Empire of Valdrek (hoards Aeridian Fragments), Guild of Threads (knows the truth about Vor'Athek), Children of the Broken Thread (radicals), Myr Council (neutral brokers).

Aeridian Fragments are immune to corruption and stabilize magic within a 50-meter radius.

Corruption without protection: fire -> burns the caster; water -> necrotizes; earth -> erodes consciousness; air -> attracts an Echo; energy -> causes a Surge; spirit -> awakens something.

Players are isekai travelers summoned by the Dome. Their secret objective is revealed gradually.
